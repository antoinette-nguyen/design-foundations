I'm Antoinette — a UI/UX designer closing the gap between what I can imagine and what I can build. I'm learning frontend development in public, one commit at a time.

Background

I've spent years working in UI/UX design — wireframing, prototyping in Figma, running user research, and handing off specs to developers. But I kept hitting the same wall: something always got lost in translation. So I decided to learn to build what I design.

What I'm building toward

A portfolio that proves I can do both — design with intention and implement with craft. Over the next 9 months I'm shipping real projects using React, Next.js, Framer Motion, and Three.js.
